OnePG is a modern payment gateway router that allows businesses to accept online payments through UPI, cards, net banking, wallets, and payment links.

Our routing engine evaluates gateway performance in real time and automatically shifts transactions to the highest-performing gateway, reducing failures and improving conversions.

You can send instant payouts to bank accounts, UPI IDs, cards, and wallets — suitable for vendor payments, refunds, salaries, partner commissions, and customer settlements.

We support Aadhaar, PAN, GSTIN, bank account verification, and more. Verification is instant, secure, and compliant with regulatory standards.
